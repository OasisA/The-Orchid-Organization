STEALTH Ideathon 2020

Students interested in using leadership & critical thinking abilities to design potential solutions to global initiatives:

On behalf of both the Lightbulb Project and Orchid Organization, we present the STEALTH Ideathon, a competition of future intellectual leaders creating solutions to a previously-selected, prospective global issue (the theme will be publicized as the event soon approaches). August CASE-X

August 14, 2020

What is a Case-X? And why should I care?

A Case-X presentation involves a patient that is exhibiting the symptoms of an undiagnosed disease.

The audience then plays the role of the physician asking questions regarding family, social, and medical history, and signs and symptoms of the ailment that the "patient" is experiencing. This is followed by them“conducting” the necessary lab tests and physical exams to ultimately diagnose the patient.

The goal of our Case-X presentations is to promote healthy habits and make our audience more cognizant of their own health to help them identify the signs and symptoms of common illnesses prior to prevent any irreversible complications. Beyond this, our Case-X presentations raise awareness about some less common illnesses to help us grow as a community and be more receptive and knowledgeable regarding these illnesses.

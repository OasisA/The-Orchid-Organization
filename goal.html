Our Goals

As a community organization, our goal is to empower our society to take the matter of their health and wellness into their own hands by providing them with the appropriate knowledge and access to resources such as free yoga sessions to accomplish this.


The Story Behind Our Name

When we were first starting out we thought naming our organization after a flower could become a symbol of wellness. Since flowers in general, and orchids particularly, can help individuals relax, reduce stress, and improve one's mood. Which can then reduce a person's risk of chronic illnesses. Also in some cultures orchids symbolize health and are believed to be a healer of many chronic illnesses. Overall we just thought the name sounded fresh and had a good ring to it, which is why we ended up going with it..
